How to Reach Us

Multiple transportation options are available to reach our school

By Car

From Diring center, take Main Road towards West Area. Turn left at GMCK college. The school is 100m on the right.

Parking: Available for visitors in front of the school.

By Bus

City buses 12, 15, and 22 stop at Community Hall, which is a 3-minute walk from the school.

Frequency: Every 15-20 minutes during school hours.

By Cycle

Cycle tracks are available on all major roads leading to the school. Secure cycle stands are provided.

Safety: Helmets are recommended for all students.
